about us

Our story begins in 1989, when the International organization of equestrian sport decided to hold competitions, combining three bright and magnificent disciplines (show-jumping, endurance riding and dressage), in the most unusual places.

our name

Why are we called WKF? Our name is the names of the most significant people for the history of equestrian sport. The date of birth of runs is 1955, when Wendell Roby and a group of riders overcame the most difficult path from Lake Tahoe to the city of Auburn (California) through the Sierra Nevada mountain range. The first letter of his name was the first letter of the name of our event Federico Kaprilli is a captain of cavalry, the founder of the natural school of riding. The new methods of working with the horse, opened to them, gave a powerful incentive to the development of jumping and equestrian triathlon, making them not only spectacular, but also technical competitions. James Fillis created an amazing book, "The Basics of Dressage and Riding," for which today he is thanked by riders from all over the world.
